# 🔧 How to Add Aksum Place to Your Website

## 📋 Quick Steps

### 1. Access Admin Panel
- Go to: https://ethiopia-361-bg5k5.sevalla.app/admin
- Login with your admin credentials

### 2. Add New Place
- Click "Add New Place" button
- Fill in the form with Aksum information (see AKSUM_PLACE_INFO.md)

### 3. Fill the Form Fields:

#### Basic Information:
- **Name:** Aksum
- **Name in Amharic:** አክሱም
- **Category:** Historical & Cultural Sites
- **Description:** (Copy from AKSUM_PLACE_INFO.md)
- **Description in Amharic:** (Copy from AKSUM_PLACE_INFO.md)

#### Media:
- **Main Image URL:** aksum_obelisk.jpg
- **Video URL:** https://youtu.be/o3euZ38gQXQ?si=uGraLaK96TIze4JT

#### Travel Guide Section:
- **Best Time to Visit:** October to March
- **How to Get There:** Fly to Aksum Airport, then take a short drive to the city
- **What to Bring:** Comfortable walking shoes, camera, sunscreen, water
- **Local Tips:** Hire a local guide to fully understand the history and significance
- **Entrance Fees:** Around 200–500 ETB for foreigners (varies by site)
- **Opening Hours:** 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
- **Accommodation:** Hotels and guesthouses available in Aksum town
- **Nearby Attractions:** St. Mary of Zion Church, Queen of Sheba's Palace, Northern Stelae Field

### 4. Upload Images (Optional)
If you want to upload actual images:
1. Place images in `/public/img/` directory:
   - aksum_obelisk.jpg
   - aksum_stelae.jpg
   - st_mary_of_zion.jpg
   - aksum_ruins.jpg
2. Or use the file upload feature in admin panel

### 5. Save
- Click "Save Place" button
- The place will be added and visible on:
  - Homepage
  - Destinations page
  - Gallery page

## 🌐 After Adding

Once added, Aksum will appear:
- ✅ On homepage with other places
- ✅ In destinations page under "Historical" category
- ✅ In gallery page with all images
- ✅ With working video embed
- ✅ With full travel guide information

## 📱 Test the Place
1. Check homepage - Aksum should be visible
2. Click "View Details" - should show full information
3. Click "Watch Video" - should play YouTube video
4. Check destinations page - should appear in Historical category
5. Check gallery page - should show all images

## 🔍 If Images Don't Show
The system will automatically use placeholder images if:
- Image files don't exist in `/public/img/`
- Image URLs are broken
- This ensures your site always looks professional
